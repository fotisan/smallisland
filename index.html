Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking.

We monitor the progress of your flight or ferry live. Our driver will be waiting for you at the exact time of your arrival, with absolutely no extra charge for the delay.
At the airport, the driver will be waiting in the arrivals hall holding a sign with your name. At the ports (Rhodes, Kamiros Skala), they will be waiting right at the disembarkation point of your ferry.
Ideally 24-48 hours in advance, so we can secure the appropriate vehicle for you. However, for last-minute transfers, send us a message on WhatsApp and we will do our best to accommodate you.
You can pay the driver directly at the end of the journey. All our vehicles have POS terminals and we accept Visa, Mastercard, Maestro cards, as well as cash.
Of course. We provide child seats and boosters free of charge. Just let us know the age and weight of the child during booking, so we can have the correct seat installed.
Our main vehicle (Audi Q5) has ample luggage space. But if you have an exceptionally large number of suitcases or special equipment, please inform us in advance so we can arrange your pickup with one of our spacious Vans.
